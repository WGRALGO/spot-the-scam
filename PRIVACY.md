# Privacy Policy — Spot the Scam

**Version 1.0.0**

Spot the Scam is built to be private by design.

- The app **does not collect personal data**.
- **No account** is required or offered.
- **No analytics**.
- **No ads**.
- **No trackers**.
- **No cloud sync**.
- **No server processing** — there is no backend.
- **No sale or sharing of data** with any third party.
- Game answers and scores are processed **locally in the app only**.
- Scores are **not permanently stored** unless persistent storage is added in a
  future version (this version does not store scores).

## Permissions

The app does **not** declare the `INTERNET` permission or any other network
permission. It cannot make network requests, load remote content, or upload
anything.

## Backups

Android cloud backup and device-to-device transfer of app data are explicitly
disabled (`allowBackup="false"` plus data-extraction/full-backup exclusion
rules).
